ShipHero: Carrier Billing & VAT Setup Guide

Third-Party Billing for Duties & Taxes

This section covers how to configure ShipHero so that duties and taxes are billed to a third-party account (e.g., the Zonos DDP account) rather than the shipper or receiver.

Supported Carriers: UPS and FedEx only. USPS and DHL are not currently supported for third-party billing in ShipHero.

UPS Setup

For Duties & Taxes Only (most common for Zonos DDP):

  1. Go to Shipping > Carriers
  2. Click Connect Carrier > select UPS
  3. Select your warehouse profile and shipping location
  4. Enter your UPS account number
  5. After the initial connection is made, go back and enter the Zonos DDP account number into the 3rd Party Billing Number for Taxes and Duties field
  6. Do NOT enable the DDP setting at the carrier level — entering the account in the duties/taxes field handles this automatically
  7. Click Connect

For Shipping + Duties/Taxes on the Same 3rd Party Account:

  1. Follow steps 1–4 above
  2. Add the 3rd Party Billing info during the connection process
  3. Enable the DDP setting
  4. No need to duplicate the info from the Shipping Charges fields into the Duties/Taxes fields
  5. Click Connect

FedEx Setup

For Duties & Taxes Only:

  1. Go to Shipping > Carriers
  2. Click Connect Carrier > select FedEx
  3. Select your profile and warehouse
  4. Enter your FedEx Account Number and Meter Number
  5. Enter the Zonos DDP account number into the Third Party Billing Number for Taxes and Duties field
  6. Click Connect

For Shipping + Duties/Taxes on the Same Account:

  1. Follow steps 1–4 above
  2. Add the 3rd Party Billing info to both the Shipping and Duties/Taxes fields
  3. Click Connect
Important: When a third-party billing account is added to the duties and taxes field, DDP is automatically applied to orders assigned that carrier profile.

Adding VAT Tax IDs

ShipHero uses Automation Rules to attach tax registration numbers (VAT, EORI, IOSS) to international orders.

Setting Up VAT via Automation Rules

  1. Navigate to app.shiphero.com > Orders > Automation Rules
  2. Click the + button to create a new rule
  3. Under Trigger, select:
    • Country > In List > select the relevant countries
    • For EU: select EU member countries
    • For UK: select from GB, England, Northern Ireland, NIR, Wales, Scotland, UK
  4. Under Action, select:
    • Set Order Tax Registration
    • Tax Type: choose from the dropdown (VAT, EORI, IOSS, etc.)
    • Tax ID: enter your tax identification number
  5. Save the rule

Tax ID Types

Type When to Use
VAT Required for doing business in the UK and EU countries. You may need separate VAT numbers per country.
EORI Used by customs to monitor and track shipments into/out of the EU.
IOSS Import One Stop Shop number — for paying VAT on imports into the EU valued under EUR 150.

Notes

  • For 3PL accounts, automation rules must be set up on the child account, not the parent
  • The appropriate Tax ID depends on: where goods ship from, the destination country, and what goods you sell
  • Each shipment to Europe requires tax registration info to be attached
